Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, cook the ground beef over medium heat until it is browned, about 5 minutes. Drain off any excess fat.
  2. Add the onion, carrot, and celery to the saucepan and cook until the vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes more.
  3. Stir in the beef broth, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Bring the sauce to a simmer and let it cook for about 15 minutes, or until it has thickened.
  4. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  5. Drain the spaghetti and return it to the pot. Add the sauce and toss to combine. Serve the spaghetti bolognese topped with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh parsley, if desired.
